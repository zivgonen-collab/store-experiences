Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 17, 2026

Who we are

STORE.com is a product comparison and review aggregation website. We help shoppers compare top-rated products across specs, pricing, and reviews so they can make confident purchasing decisions. When you click a retailer link on our site, we may earn a commission from the resulting purchase.

Information we collect

When you visit STORE.com, we automatically collect certain information about your session and how you interact with our pages, including:

  • Pages viewed and time spent on each page
  • Scroll depth and on-page interactions (e.g., product cards viewed, buttons clicked)
  • Whether you clicked through to a retailer link and when
  • Device type, browser type, and operating system
  • Referring source (e.g., the ad or search result that brought you here)
  • A randomly generated session identifier — this is not linked to your name or personal identity

We do not collect your name, email address, payment information, or any other directly identifying personal information unless you contact us directly.

Cookies and tracking technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to operate and improve the site and to measure the effectiveness of our advertising. Specifically:

  • Meta Pixel: We use the Meta Pixel to measure the performance of ads we run on Facebook and Instagram, and to build audiences for future advertising. Meta may use this data in accordance with its own Data Policy.
  • Google Ads / Google Analytics tags: We use Google's advertising and analytics tags to track conversions from our Google Ads campaigns and to understand how visitors use the site. You can opt out of Google's personalized advertising via Google Ads Settings.
  • First-party session cookies: We set short-lived cookies to maintain your session state while you browse the site. These expire when you close your browser.

How we use the information

We use the data we collect to:

  • Improve the quality and relevance of product recommendations on the site
  • Measure and optimize the performance of our advertising campaigns
  • Detect and filter invalid or fraudulent traffic to protect the integrity of our platform
  • Analyze engagement patterns to understand which content is most useful to visitors

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

Affiliate relationships

STORE.com participates in affiliate advertising programs. When you click a product link on our site and make a purchase, we may earn a commission at no additional cost to you. Affiliate relationships do not influence our product recommendations or editorial coverage.

Your privacy rights (California residents)

If you are a California resident, you have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information we have collected about you, request deletion of that information, and opt out of the sale of your personal information (we do not sell personal information).

To exercise any of these rights, please email us at opt-out@admedia.com. We will respond within 45 days.

Data retention

Session and behavioral data is retained for up to 24 months and then deleted or anonymized. We retain aggregated, non-identifiable analytics data indefinitely.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of the site after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, contact us at support@admedia.com.
